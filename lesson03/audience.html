AUDIENCE

The main target audiences are entrepreneurs and franchisers looking to build a business in the area.

PERSONA 1

The Entrepreneur:

  • Age: 25+ years old
  • Sex: Male
  • College-educated and full of youthful motivation.
  • Needs to strike some deals and set up a home base.
  • Is looking for as much free advertising as possible.
  • “We think we are offering something this community has really been asking for. The thing is, not everybody knows we’re here and open for business!”

PERSONA 2

The Franchiser:

  • Age: 35+ years old
  • Sex: Female
  • Experienced business manager looking to leverage a proven business model.
  • Needs information on demographics and logistics in the area.
  • Is looking for ways to reach contractors and consumers, and wants to increase general visibility and confidence within the community.
  • “If the success of our nearby franchises is any indication, we know this is something members of this community want. We’re super excited to save them a drive and bring our offerings right here to them.”

SCENARIOS

  • Activities and Events
  • Demographics
  • Weather
  • Business News
  • Local Pictures
